Module 2 Video Reflection - Five Questions

1. How does the role of Cinderella's mother change across adaptations, and what does that reveal about morality and belief?

2. How does Cinderella's level of agency change across versions, and what does that reflect about evolving ideas of strength?

3. What does the ash symbolize in Cinderella, especially in the 2015 version where it represents a loss of identity?

4. What is the significance of Cinderella saying, 'Just because it's what's done doesn't mean it's what should be done,' and how does that reflect modern values?

5. How do the different endings reflect shifting cultural ideas about justice, love, identity, and reward?
