## üìö Key Questions Explored

1. How does the role of Cinderella's mother differ across adaptations, and what does that reveal about morality and belief?
2. How does Cinderella's level of agency change, reflecting evolving ideas of strength in women?
3. What does the stepsisters' self-mutilation symbolize about social pressure and women's worth?
4. How do different approaches to justice and accountability appear across versions?
5. How do the different endings communicate shifting cultural values around love, identity, and reward?
